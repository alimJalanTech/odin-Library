Library Management System

To add a book to the library, follow these steps: Start by filling in the form with the details of your book: Book Name: Enter your Book's title Author: Enter the name of your author Number of Pages: Enter the total number of pages of your book Have You Read This?

Check the box if you have read this book. Click the "Submit" button to submit the form. Your book will now be added to the Library! You can view it in the list below along with any other books that have been added. To remove a book or change its read status, click on the appropriate button next to the book. That's it! You can go ahead and add as many books as you want. Happy reading!
